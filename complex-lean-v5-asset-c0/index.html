Your card is a serial — № 00,000,000 through whatever the press cuts to next. It is your line at every drop, your seat at every Q&A, and the only proof of your tenure when you leave. Foil-stamped. Mailed once.

Why is Obsessed the middle tier and the only one featured?
Because that's where the obsessed actually live — past the free reader, short of the household. Foil. Numbered. The middle is the point.

Can I cancel?
Anytime. Your card goes back to the press. Your seat goes back to the line. Nothing is held against you on the record.
004
When do drops happen?
Quietly. We do not announce. Members are messaged first; the rest of the world finds out when the run is over. Median sell-out: 96 seconds.
