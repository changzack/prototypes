The Counter

David Tsan, chef · eight seats · chef-led service
板前

David Tsan cooks from the working side of the kappo counter: fish set down in the order it should be cut, rice held warm enough to breathe, soy brushed only when the bite asks for it.

Counter seats, eight at a time. The chef chooses the sequence, but the ledger is visible: origin, hour, texture, flame. The meal is not announced as luxury; it is proven by attention.
