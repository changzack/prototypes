THE MANIFESTO.

"WE BUILT THIS FOR THE ONES WHO ALREADY KNOW."

Allegiance isn't a discount code. It's a position. You either show up for the music, the streetwear, the films, the long-form sit-downs — or you don't. We're not going to convince you. We're going to ship to the ones who already do.

Pallet 09 is the ninth wave of members we've signed since we started numbering them. Every wave gets a card. Every card is a receipt for being early.

If you've been reading Complex since the print magazine, you already know. If you started last week, welcome — same rate, same crate, same rank.

Can I cancel?

Any time. One click. We don't do retention dark patterns. Your card stays yours — it's been stamped, you keep the number.

Because we treat what we ship like cargo. Numbered. Inventoried. On a manifest. You're not "joining a community" — you're on a list with a serial number.

It is mailed. Bone cardstock, ink-black border, your number embossed in the corner. Allow 14 days domestic, 28 international.

Five seats and two event tickets a year. Bring your group chat. One seat is giftable — birthday move locked in.

Q.07
Will Regular ever stop being free?

No. The newsletter and the open archive stay open. The line moves; the door doesn't close.

Q.08
Where's my receipt?

In your email and on your member dashboard. Bill of lading format. We print it like cargo paperwork because that's what it is.
