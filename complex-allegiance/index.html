// 04 — Intel

Things you'll probably ask.

Is the regular site still free? +

Yes. Forever. Every story, every video, every list — free. Allegiance is for the obsessed who want to go deeper, not a paywall around what we already make.

What's a "drop" actually look like? +

Members get a 24-hour head start on every limited Complex product — tees, hoodies, pins, vinyl, zines, collabs with the artists and brands we cover. Numbered runs. When it's gone, it's gone. Median sell-out time for the last 12 drops: 96 seconds.

What's on the monthly intel drop? +

A members-only newsletter from Complex editors covering what's about to pop — songs, sneakers, shows, fits, tech — before the press release. Plus pre-publish access to our biggest features. Sent on the first of every month.

Can I cancel? +

One tap, anywhere in your account. No retention call. No "are you sure." You can come back whenever — you'll just lose your founding member rate.

Why does this cost less than your competitors? +

Because Complex doesn't need to make Allegiance the business. We have one. This is the deeper room for people who want it. We're not trying to gate the front door, we're opening a side one.

Is the card real? +

Yes. Numbered, foil-stamped, mailed to you within 14 days of signing up. There's a digital one in your account that does the work; the physical one is for the wallet.
