SAYRE № 01

Distilled in November.
Bottled in stone.

A scent constructed around the cold mineral memory of Avignon's cathedral stones, warmed by the human trace of incense, designed to unfold gradually as it wears.

It begins as a sharp, atmospheric mist—bergamot and fig leaf catching the chill—before settling into a dense, tactile heart of iris and smoked vetiver. The shift is deliberate, moving from pure air to heavy earth.

The base notes act as an anchor, tethering the ethereal upper layers to the skin with raw labdanum and ambrette seed. A formula not for the crowd, but strictly for the wearer.
